About Trickle Cash

Trickle Cash is a continuous budget tracking app that helps you manage your money more effectively. Instead of thinking about your budget in fixed monthly amounts, Trickle Cash shows you a "virtual wallet" that fills up gradually throughout the month.

Key Features

  • Continuous Budget: Enter your monthly budget and watch your virtual wallet grow in real-time
  • Expense Tracking: Easily record your spending, which is automatically deducted from your virtual wallet
  • Savings Buckets: Create dedicated savings buckets for future purchases or goals

When to Contact Support
